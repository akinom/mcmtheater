Actors form the core of the mcmTheater package. They have shapes, move about and may leave a trail along their movement path. The actors below are simple examples, move and turn commands can be used to draw some simple shapes.

The applets below run one actor each. All actors start with default colors and appear at the center of the applet window showing a default shape, a circle that contains a line to indicate movement direction. They exceute in debug mode, which slows turning and moving.
