The first step in transforming the square drawing actor is to undertand it. It simply calls the square method once in its act method which it executes as soon at it enters the Stage. In this case that means as soon as it shows up in the applet's window below. Every occurance of the variable size in the square method is replaced by the number 50 when it is executed. Therefore square(50); moves 4 times 50 pixels forward following each move by a 90 degree turn to the right.
