The actors below draw triangles in different sizes. The triangle on the left has an edge length of 40 pixels. The edges in the middle are 60 pixels long and on the right they are 90 pixels long. When you compare the shape methods of the three actors you see that they differ in the number they pass to their move calls:

  • The left actor passes 40,
  • the middle 60, and
  • the right the variable size which receives the value 90 when the left actor calls shape(90) in its act method.
The examples on the following pages continue to use the right actor's shape method in order to draw triangles of various sizes.
