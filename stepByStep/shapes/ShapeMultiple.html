Drawing the same triangle at the same position over and over again is not all that exciting. To make the drawn shapes more exciting we can draw differently sized triangles, rotate the triangles, and draw them at different positions.

The actors below use the previously defined shape method to draw multiple triangles. The first draws differently sized triangles, the second rotates the triangles around a center, and the third draws triangles at randomly chosen positions.

The shows actors that combine differently sized rotated triangles into more sophisticated patterns. Actors that draw a series of triangles along a line can be seen at Sliding Traingles. Actors that randomly jump or move around the stage to fill it with triangles are shown at Jumping Triangles.
