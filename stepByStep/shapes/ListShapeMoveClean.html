makeMover creates a new ShapeCleanMoveInside actor and assigns a reference to this new actor to the variable mover. It sets the new actor's trail width to 2 pixels, its position to the stage's center, its shape to a new filled EllipseShape shape with a 9 pixel diameter, its shape's color and its trail color to the color value given when the makeMover method is called. makeMover starts the new actor in its last statement. Note the use of mover. in makeMover's statements. This prefix is necessary to make explicit that the new mover's trail width, shape, ... is to be changed not the initial actor's trail width, shape, ...
